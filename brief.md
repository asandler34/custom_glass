# Exquisite Custom Glass Showers — Website Brief

## Business Overview
Local premium glass fabrication and installation company serving Massachusetts and New Hampshire.
Specializes in custom shower glass enclosures, frameless glass doors, glass railings, custom mirrors,
and bespoke interior glass projects for high-end residential and commercial clients.

## Core Differentiators
- Fully custom — no off-the-shelf products
- Precision fabrication and white-glove installation
- Works with architects, designers, and homeowners
- Premium materials with decades of craftsmanship behind every project

## Target Audience
- Homeowners doing high-end bathroom or home renovations ($500K+ homes)
- Interior designers and architects specifying materials for clients
- Contractors working on premium builds in MA and NH

## Emotional Tone to Convey
- Trust and permanence — this is not a purchase, it's an investment
- Artisan quality — made by hand, fitted to your space
- Clean, modern luxury — not ostentatious, but clearly premium
- Local and reliable — real people, real craftsmanship, real accountability

## Pages Required
1. Home (hero, intro, services overview, process, gallery teaser, testimonials, CTA, footer)
2. Services (shower doors, railings, mirrors, commercial glass — each with detail)
3. Gallery / Portfolio (filterable project grid)

- "Request a Free Estimate" (primary — appears everywhere)
